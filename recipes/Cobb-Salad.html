A Cobb salad is a classic and easy-to-make American dish involving common household ingredients.

Ingredients

  • Choice of meat, often bacon or chicken
  • Boiled eggs
  • Lettuce (generally, Iceberg lettuce is used)
  • Tomatoes (Sliced or cherry)
  • Blue cheese crumbles
  • Green onions
  • Avocado
  • Dressing

Step-By-Step

  1. Boil, peel, and slice the eggs into several pieces based on preference.
  2. Cook the bacon until crisp, drain the fat, and crumple over the salad.
  3. Assemble the ingredients into a bowl and gently toss.
  4. Use whatever dressing you prefer, and serve.
  1. Prep Time: 20 minutes
  2. Cook Time 10 minutes
  3. Total Time: 30 minutes
  4. Servings: 6
