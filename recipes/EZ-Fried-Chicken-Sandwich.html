Classic and easy fried chicken sandwich

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 4 skinless, boneless chicken breasts
  • 5 tablespoons vegetable oil (adjust as needed)
  • 4 leaves romaine lettuce
  • 1 large tomato, slice to preference
  • 4 whole wheat buns, toasted

Step-By-Step

  1. Boil, peel, and slice the eggs into several pieces based on preference.
  2. Cook the bacon until crisp, drain the fat, and crumple over the salad.
  3. Assemble the ingredients into a bowl and gently toss.
  1. Prep Time: 10 minutes
  2. Cook Time 10 minutes
  3. Total Time: 20 minutes
  4. Servings: 4
