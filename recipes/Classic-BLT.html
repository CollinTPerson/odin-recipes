Simple BLT

Ingredients

  • 4 slices of bacon
  • 2 leaves of iceberg lettuce
  • 2 tomatoes, sliced to preference
  • 2 toasted slices of bread
  • 1 tablespoon of mayonnaise

Step-By-Step

  1. Gather all of the ingredients you've prepared.
  2. Cook bacon until evenly browned, and drain excess grease by laying slices on a paper towel.
  3. Spread the mayonnaise on one slice of bread, and layer the ingredients on the other.
  4. Close the sandwich and serve.
  1. Prep Time: 5 minutes
  2. Cook Time 10 minutes
  3. Total Time: 15 minutes
  4. Servings: 1
